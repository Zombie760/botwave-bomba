# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

### Behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- **Empathy** — Demonstrating kindness toward others
- **Respect** — Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **Constructive feedback** — Giving and gracefully accepting actionable feedback
- **Accountability** — Accepting responsibility for mistakes, apologizing, and learning
- **Focus on the work** — Prioritizing what's best for the project and community

### Behavior that is unacceptable:

- **Harassment** — Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal attacks, sustained disruption
- **Discrimination** — Exclusionary language, stereotypes, or actions based on protected characteristics
- **Sexual content** — Unwelcome sexual advances, sexualized language, or imagery
- **Doxxing** — Publishing private information without explicit consent
- **Threats** — Physical or legal threats, intimidation
- **Spam/self-promotion** — Unrelated commercial promotion, crypto shilling, etc.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (GitHub repos, issues, PRs, Discussions, Discord/Telegram if any) and also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at **conduct@botwave.io**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

All maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining consequences:

### 1. Correction
**Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcoming.
**Consequence:** Private written warning, clarification of violation. Public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning
**Impact:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions.
**Consequence:** Warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with reporter for a specified period. Includes project spaces and external channels.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact:** Serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.
**Consequence:** Temporary ban from all project spaces for a specified period. No public or private interaction with maintainers/contributors.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact:** Pattern of harassment, aggression toward individuals, or discrimination against protected groups.
**Consequence:** Permanent ban from all project spaces and public representation of the project.
